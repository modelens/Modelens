Client: Sourin India handbags Photoshoot
Project includes: 10 models, 2 professional photographers, 1 makeup artist
Date: January 2023

The Problem

Sourin India wanted to establish a strong presence on Instagram for their bags. The goal was to create visually striking content that would capture the brand's innovative spirit and showcase their products effectively using different models and their various different styles and coloured bags within limited time frame. The challenge was to develop a photoshoot that not only highlighted the bags but also appealed to a broad Instagram audience.

The Solution

Modern and Trendy Visuals:
We designed a photoshoot that aligns with Sourin India’s brand aesthetics. By selecting trendy and contemporary settings, the clean and bold imagery ensured the focus remained on the bags.

Diverse Model Representation:
We incorporated a diverse group of models to represent the inclusivity of the brand. This approach ensured that the audience could see themselves using Sourin India’s products.

High-Quality Product Shots:
High-resolution images showcased the bags from multiple angles, providing followers with a clear and detailed view of the products. This strategy helped highlight the unique features of each bag.

Interactive and Engaging Content:
To create an immediate impact, we used dynamic and engaging visual elements, including short videos and GIFs, to draw attention and provide a unique browsing experience.

Clear Call to Action:
Each post included strategically placed call-to-action buttons encouraging followers to explore the products and make purchases directly through Instagram. The seamless integration ensured an easy shopping experience.

Consistent and Cohesive Theme:
The photoshoot maintained a consistent and cohesive theme across all posts, creating a strong and recognizable brand identity on Instagram.

The Result

Increased Engagement:
The engaging visuals and interactive content significantly improved user engagement, keeping followers on the page longer. The visually appealing design led to higher interaction rates.

Higher Conversion Rates:
The clear and compelling call-to-action buttons led to a higher number of clicks and conversions. Detailed product images helped reduce hesitation among potential buyers, leading to increased sales.

Positive Brand Perception:
The modern and innovative photoshoot enhanced Sourin India’s brand image, positioning them as a forward-thinking and creative brand. Followers associated the high-quality visuals and interactive elements with the premium quality of the bags.

Broader Audience Reach:
The diverse model representation and optimized posts for mobile devices ensured a wider audience could access and interact with the content, leading to a broader customer base and increased brand recognition on Instagram.
